🎯 Job Title: UI/UX Designer
📍 Location

Mumbai / Remote / Hybrid

🧠 Role Overview

We’re looking for a UI/UX Designer who can turn complex ideas into intuitive, clean, and engaging digital experiences. You’ll work closely with product managers, developers, and stakeholders to design user-centered solutions that actually make sense (unlike half the apps out there 👀).

🛠️ Responsibilities

Design user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity UI screens

Conduct user research, usability testing, and gather feedback

Collaborate with developers to ensure designs are implemented correctly

Improve existing product experiences based on data and user behavior

Maintain and evolve design systems and UI consistency

Translate business requirements into user-friendly designs

Stay updated with design trends, tools, and best practices

🧩 Requirements

Bachelor’s degree in Design, HCI, or related field (or equivalent skills)

1–4 years of experience in UI/UX or product design

Strong portfolio showcasing real projects (not just Dribbble fantasies 😏)

Proficiency in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, or similar

Understanding of UX principles, accessibility, and responsive design

Basic knowledge of HTML/CSS is a plus

Strong communication and problem-solving skills

⭐ Good to Have

Experience working in agile teams

Familiarity with user analytics tools (Google Analytics, Hotjar, etc.)

Motion design or micro-interaction experience

Experience with mobile app design

🎁 What We Offer

Competitive salary

Flexible working hours / hybrid setup

Opportunity to work on real products (not just “coming soon” pages)

Learning and growth opportunities

Chill team culture (hopefully not “we’re a family” toxic edition)
