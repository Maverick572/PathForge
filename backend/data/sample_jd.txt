Job Title: Mid-Level Machine Learning Engineer
Company: TechCorp India

About the Role:
We are looking for a Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI team.
You will build and deploy ML models that power our recommendation engine.

Required Skills and Experience:
- Strong proficiency in Python and experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Experience with NLP techniques including transformers and BERT
- Knowledge of MLflow for experiment tracking and model registry
- Experience with Kubernetes and Docker for model deployment
- Familiarity with Kafka for real-time data pipelines
- SQL and experience with data warehouses such as Snowflake or BigQuery
- Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices
- Experience with feature stores such as Feast or Tecton

Nice to Have:
- Knowledge of Spark or Dask for large-scale data processing
- Experience with Airflow for pipeline orchestration
- Familiarity with Hugging Face transformers library
- Knowledge of vector databases such as Pinecone or Weaviate

Education:
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
